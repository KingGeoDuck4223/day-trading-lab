## Standing rule reminders to self
- Losing money is the clearest signal something is wrong. A loss without a specific,
  concrete reason attached is a wasted day — don't let "the market was just down" be
  the whole explanation if a sharper one is available.
- Don't keep running a strategy on autopilot just because it's not actively losing.
  Every 5th trading day, deliberately test something different even if the current
  approach is working — the goal is to find out if it still holds up, not to protect
  a winning streak.
- If a strategy has two losing days in a row, stop using it and either revise it with
  a stated reason or retire it. Don't repeat a discredited approach without a specific
  reason to think conditions have changed.
